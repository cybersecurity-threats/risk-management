Examining evolving cybersecurity threats in both real-world environments and social media to design a Holistic Risk Management Framework (HRMF) integrating Technical, Behavioral, and Social Media Exposure factors.

Holistic Risk Management Framework (HRMF)

Behavioral Factors

Digital impulsivity, cognitive biases, and psychological susceptibility affecting online decision-making and exposure to phishing or manipulation.

Technical Factors

Security tools like Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), software updates, and AI-driven detection systems enhancing defensive capability.

Social Media Exposure

Oversharing, weak privacy settings, and extensive digital footprints that amplify personal and organizational cybersecurity risk.
