SENIOR RESEARCH PROJECT 2026

Cybersecurity Threats and Risk Management in real-world and social media

A comprehensive research focused on a Holistic Risk Management Framework (HRMF) to bridge the gap between public awareness and evolving digital threats.

OUR MODEL

The HRMF Framework

Technical Factors

Analyzing defensive capabilities through Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), AI-driven detection, and system updates.

Behavioral Factors

Measuring user susceptibility to phishing and password hygiene patterns under digital threat.

Social Media Exposure

Evaluating how oversharing on digital platforms acts as a mediating variable for cyber risk.
